About

Vi Saint is a remote-first designer & developer with an unconventional background spanning multiple industries & creative disciplines. He specializes in turning complexity into intuitive & emotionally resonant experiences that feel just right.

With 5+ years crafting everything from video game interfaces to high-converting e-commerce platforms, Viktor brings a rare mix: technical chops, cultural intelligence & obsessive attention to the human computer interaction.

He doesn't build generic solutions—he creates brand worlds that feel alive, resonant across channels & highly memorable.
