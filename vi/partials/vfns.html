UI/UX Designer Indie Game Studio

A video game studio with a narrative bent. I joined the small team and instantly started wearing multiple hats, learning on the fly & contributing across the board.

I joined to create understandable & expected User Interfaces for video games.

Got interested in the full Player Experience, contributed to video game stories, pacing, unlocks and skill progression, playtesting every build and conducting playtest groups.

Expanded to asset creation, drawing pixels and scavenging for the perfect texture to bring the world to life.

Designed the website & played around with the brand until landing on the perfect system.
