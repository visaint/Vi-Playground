Graphic Designer Anxiety Book

I led the design and creative direction for a complete brand identity and a flagship print book focused on mental wellness.

Color & Illustration: I developed a palette of pleasing colors—soft, desaturated tones—that evoke tranquility and calm. This was paired with calming and descriptive illustrations that translate complex emotional or psychological concepts into easily digestible, supportive visuals.

Typography & Layout: I selected elegant typography that is approachable yet sophisticated, prioritizing legibility and comfort. The layout was engineered for an uninterrupted reading flow, with ample white space and considered hierarchy to prevent visual fatigue, ensuring the content is the priority.

Brand Identity System: I created a complete visual identity (logo, brand guidelines, digital assets) that maintains consistency across all touchpoints, ensuring the brand voice is consistently supportive, clear, and serene.
